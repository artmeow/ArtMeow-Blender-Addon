## ⚠️ Limitations & Feature Comparison
## ⚠️ 試用版功能限制與對比

This trial version is for workflow evaluation only and does not represent the full capabilities or complete user experience of the paid version. The differences are detailed below:
本試用版僅作為功能流程的評估，並不代表完整版插件的全部效能與完整體驗。試用版與完整付費版的功能差異如下：

* **PBR Channels Support**
  * **Demo Version**: Supports **only 2 channels** (BaseColor and Normal).
  * **Full Version**: Supports **all 9 channels** (BaseColor, Normal, Metallic, Roughness, Specular, AO, MRAO, Height, Emissive).
* **PBR 通道支援**
  * **試用版 (Demo)**：**僅限 2 個** (BaseColor / Normal)。
  * **完整版 (Full)**：**支援所有 9 大通道** (BaseColor, Normal, Metallic, Roughness, Specular, AO, MRAO, Height, Emissive)。

* **Channel Keywords Settings (S2)**
  * **Demo Version**: Only displays and allows editing for the 2 core channels.
  * **Full Version**: Allows full customization of keyword matching for all 9 channels.
* **通道關鍵字設定 (S2)**
  * **試用版 (Demo)**：僅顯示與編輯 2 個核心通道。
  * **完整版 (Full)**：可自由自訂 9 大通道的對應關鍵字。

* **Smart Shader Creation (S3)**
  * **Demo Version**: Automatically connects only Base Color and Normal map nodes.
  * **Full Version**: Automatically generates and wires all matching PBR textures (including MRAO channel splitting and AO multiply mixing nodes).
* **智能 Shader 建立 (S3)**
  * **試用版 (Demo)**：僅會建立基礎顏色與法線連線。
  * **完整版 (Full)**：自動建立並混合所有 PBR 貼圖與進階節點（如 MRAO 自動拆圖與 AO 疊加等）。

* **Name Batch Matching (S1 & S4)**
  * **Demo Version**: ❌ Disabled.
  * **Full Version**: One-click automatic matching and pairing between scene Meshes and multiple texture sets.
* **主名稱批次配對 (S1 & S4)**
  * **試用版 (Demo)**：❌ 未啟用。
  * **完整版 (Full)**：一鍵自動進行 Mesh 與多套材質貼圖的精密批次配對。

* **Updates & Support**
  * **Demo Version**: ❌ None.
  * **Full Version**: Lifetime free updates and technical customer support.
* **售後支援與更新**
  * **試用版 (Demo)**：❌ 無。
  * **完整版 (Full)**：終身免費更新與技術支援。
